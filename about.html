About Us

Founded by owner, John Kenner, in 1984, Tamalpais is a moderately-sized cabinet shop that has grown at a careful and considered rate. We have built our business and our team around a core group of skilled, long-term employees. Our 25,000 SF shop in Richmond is outfitted with state-of-the art woodworking equipment. Tamalpais is a member of the Woodwork Institute and a Union Shop.

Core Values

  • Service
  • Professionalism/Integrity/Commitment/Honesty/Reliability
  • Attention to Details
  • Teamwork
  • Accountability
  • Quality

Our goal is not to be the biggest, but to provide the best service in commercial cabinetry in the San Francisco Bay Area.
