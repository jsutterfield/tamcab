What We Do

We are a team of project managers, draftsmen, craftsmen and skilled installers that take projects from concept to construction. We pay attention to the details of cabinet construction and installation; we consider how the millwork will be used, its location, and associated equipment & mechanical requirements.

Specializing In

  • Commercial/Tenant Improvements
  • Medical Facilities & Offices
  • Banks
  • Private Institutions

The majority of our work is from repeat clients that know they can give us the millwork package and forget about it. We approach each project as a partnership with the General Contractor and the Architect. We provide value-engineering options that maintain design integrity and meet budget constraints. We work with our clients to resolve scheduling issues, changes in field conditions, and last minute modifications.

Tamalpais' "Zero Punch-List Approach" to installation ensures that our cabinets are installed right the first time, maintaining the project schedule with a minimum of interference to other trades. Our clients can rest assured that should there be any complications, "We will take care of it!"
